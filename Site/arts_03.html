Using 3dSMAX, I created two planets with the appearance of occludable terrain controlled by the displacement of the materials. I applied specularity and self illuminating materials to each of the spheres adding the illusion that the planets glow from within. The nebulous star field background gives the planets a formidable appearance. I created this project to learn to use materials to build the 3d displacement map seen on both planets.
